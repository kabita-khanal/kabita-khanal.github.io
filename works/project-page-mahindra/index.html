Agni | Mahindra

Brand Identity & Campaign Design

Project Overview

Agni Group, the authorized distributor of Mahindra vehicles, wanted to create a brand campaign that highlights the strength, reliability, and innovation of their vehicles. The goal was to create an impactful campaign that reflects the brand’s dominance in Nepal’s auto industry.

Challenges

  • Developing a unique visual identity while maintaining Mahindra’s global branding.
  • Creating marketing materials for digital, print, and outdoor media.
  • Designing a campaign that resonates with Nepal’s diverse audience.

Our Solution

We developed a bold and dynamic visual language integrating Mahindra’s signature red and strong typography. The campaign featured high-energy visuals that emphasized durability, adventure, and innovation.

Results & Impact

The campaign generated **high engagement** on social media, with a **30% increase in user interaction**. Sales inquiries increased by **20%**, and the billboard designs received positive recognition from the client.

Client Testimonial

"The design work exceeded our expectations! The campaign truly captured Mahindra’s essence and helped us connect with our audience in a powerful way."
— Marketing Head, Agni Group
